MECHANICS TEAM

At the beginning of the new season the Mechanics Team is busy designing the robot in professional software. As they finish their work, they create a list of needed components, which afterwards is given to the Accounting section. Then they start printing particular parts using 3D printers. In January when the ordered elements arrive, the most interesting part begins - screwing screws! Mechanics drive around the city, buy various bits and bobs necessary to build a robot according to the previously created plan.

ACCOUNTING

Accounting is mainly responsible for supervising and managing our team’s budget. They keep a record of expenses and needs of our team on an ongoing basis. They make sure that all funds allocated by sponsors are used in the best possible way. They look for offers which both have fair prices and great quality of products. Afterwards based on the list, previously made by the Mechanics Section, members of the Accounting Section order needed parts, components and materials to build a robot.
